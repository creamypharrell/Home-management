Perth Climate — Seasonal Task Adjustments
Summer — Dec to Feb
Currently active: No
Mow lawn weekly. Water early morning only. Increase dust cleaning frequency. Check ceiling fans and air vents. 40°C+ days — avoid outdoor tasks; reschedule to early morning.
Autumn — Mar to May
Currently active: Yes
Wind increases leaf and debris buildup — sweep patio more often. Gutter check recommended before end of season. Begin reducing lawn watering as temps drop.
Winter — Jun to Aug
Currently active: No
Reduce lawn mowing to fortnightly. Increase indoor cleaning focus. Check bathrooms and wet areas for mould. Review garden bed drainage.
Spring — Sep to Nov
Currently active: No
Resume weekly mowing schedule. Weed surge in garden beds — increase weeding cadence. Pest check recommended (ants, spiders). Refresh outdoor furniture.
